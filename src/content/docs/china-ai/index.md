# China AI

China’s AI story is too often reduced to a single scoreboard: which model is closest to the American frontier, which app has the most users, which company has the best benchmark, which chip ban is most binding. Those comparisons matter, but they miss the more interesting layer.

The Chinese AI market is becoming a test bed for three questions at once: how to make intelligence cheaper, how to turn assistant usage into real commercial transactions, and how to design products that live inside a user’s actual work or life environment.
