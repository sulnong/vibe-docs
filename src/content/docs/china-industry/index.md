# China Industry

China’s industrial story is not only about cheap factories or export volume. It is increasingly about knowledge, engineering density, supply-chain coordination, overseas production, automation, energy, and the ability to turn manufacturing experience into global operating systems.

This topic follows how Chinese industry moves from “factory for the world” toward something more complex: a network that combines domestic engineering, global labor, local-market production, and new technology infrastructure.
